# Sales 2025 Project Details

4️⃣ Overall Sales Performance Dashboard

A consolidated sales dashboard showing overall business performance across regions, categories, and order priorities. It visualizes revenue trends by month and region, order distribution, and top-performing products, offering a complete executive-level overview.

1️⃣ Sales Report – Region West (its Drillthrough Page from Region in above Overall Sales Performance Dashboard)

An interactive sales performance dashboard focused on the West region, highlighting key KPIs such as Customers, Revenue, Shipping Cost, Orders, and Profit. It includes category-wise sales distribution, monthly revenue trends, product-level insights, and identification of the highest-selling product to support regional decision-making.

2️⃣ Revenue Time Intelligence Report

A time-based analytical report built using Time Intelligence DAX, providing insights into MoM growth, QTD, YTD, previous month revenue, and rolling revenues (10-day & 90-day). This report helps track revenue trends across years, quarters, and months with strong comparative analysis.

3️⃣ Calculation Groups & Field Selector Report

A dynamic Power BI report leveraging Calculation Groups and Field Parameters to switch seamlessly between measures such as Orders.

PIZZA Sales Project Details
1. Tools and Technologies Used

Power BI Desktop – for data modeling and visualization

Power Query – for data cleaning and transformation

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – for creating calculated measures

Data Modeling – to establish relationships between tables

2. Data Processing and Modeling

Before visualization, the data undergoes several preprocessing steps:

Removal of duplicate records

Handling missing and inconsistent values

Creation of calculated columns and measures

Establishment of relationships between fact and dimension tables

These steps ensure data accuracy and improve report performance.

3. Dashboard Design and Visualization Theory
3.1 KPI Cards

KPI cards are used to display key performance indicators such as Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, and Total Pizzas Sold.
These visuals provide a quick and clear summary of overall business performance.

3.2 Line and Area Charts

Line and area charts are used to analyze revenue trends over time.
They help in identifying seasonality, growth patterns, and periods of high or low sales.

3.3 Bar Charts

Bar charts are used to compare revenue across pizza categories and individual pizza types.
They allow easy comparison and help identify high-performing and low-performing products.

3.4 Donut and Pie Charts

Donut charts are used to represent proportional data such as:

Revenue by pizza category

Orders by pizza size

Weekday vs weekend sales

These charts help visualize distribution and customer preferences.

3.5 Gauge Charts

Gauge charts are used to compare actual performance against predefined targets for revenue and orders.
They help assess whether business goals are being met.

3.6 Tables and Matrix Visuals

Tables and matrix visuals are used for detailed, row-level analysis of pizzas, including total orders and revenue.
These visuals support drill-down analysis and operational insights.

4. Sales Performance Analysis (Theoretical Interpretation)
4.1 Monthly Sales Analysis

Monthly sales analysis helps identify seasonal demand patterns.
Higher sales during certain months indicate peak demand periods, while lower sales suggest opportunities for promotional strategies.

4.2 Category-wise Analysis

Category-wise analysis shows how different pizza categories contribute to total revenue.
A balanced contribution indicates a diversified product portfolio, reducing dependency on a single category.

4.3 Pizza-wise Analysis

Pizza-wise analysis identifies best-selling and least-selling pizzas.
This information is useful for menu optimization and inventory management.

4.4 Customer Ordering Behavior

Analysis of pizza size preference and weekday vs weekend sales provides insights into customer behavior.
Understanding these patterns helps businesses design targeted marketing and pricing strategies.
