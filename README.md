# Power-BI-Project

4️⃣ Overall Sales Performance Dashboard

A consolidated sales dashboard showing overall business performance across regions, categories, and order priorities. It visualizes revenue trends by month and region, order distribution, and top-performing products, offering a complete executive-level overview.

1️⃣ Sales Report – Region West (its Drillthrough Page from Region in above Overall Sales Performance Dashboard)

An interactive sales performance dashboard focused on the West region, highlighting key KPIs such as Customers, Revenue, Shipping Cost, Orders, and Profit. It includes category-wise sales distribution, monthly revenue trends, product-level insights, and identification of the highest-selling product to support regional decision-making.

2️⃣ Revenue Time Intelligence Report

A time-based analytical report built using Time Intelligence DAX, providing insights into MoM growth, QTD, YTD, previous month revenue, and rolling revenues (10-day & 90-day). This report helps track revenue trends across years, quarters, and months with strong comparative analysis.

3️⃣ Calculation Groups & Field Selector Report

A dynamic Power BI report leveraging Calculation Groups and Field Parameters to switch seamlessly between measures such as Orders, Revenue, Profit, and Shipping Cost. Enables flexible analysis of MOM, QTD, and YTD metrics without duplicating measures.
